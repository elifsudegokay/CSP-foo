This page allows you to edit one of three files, depending on the kind of book you have.

  • assignment.rst -- add custom problems or questions
  • toc.rst -- if your book is based on How to Think Like a Computer Scientist, then this will let you customize the order of the content.
  • index.rst -- for all other books this will allow you to customize the order of the contents. For thinkcspy books this will let you change the landing page.

Use this editing page carefully, mistakes here can cause errors in the rebuild process which will break your course.
